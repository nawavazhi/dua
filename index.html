How it works

Learn the way it was meant to be taught

Classical Islamic scholarship always taught prayer word by word. This project brings that tradition online — at no cost, with no ads, for everyone.

01
Read word by word
Each word of every dua is shown alongside its transliteration, English meaning, and Malayalam translation in a clean table.
02
Hear the pronunciation
Tap any Arabic word in the Quran surahs to hear it pronounced clearly by a reciter — straight from QuranCDN.
03
Track what you know
Mark each section as memorised. Your progress is saved locally on your device — no account, no tracking, no data shared.
